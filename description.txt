Vanilla Calamity Mod Music is an in-progress music mod that aims to replace the entire vanilla soundtrack with new songs made in the context of the Calamity Mod OST.

-= Credits =-

Music: Heart Plus Up!, SkippyZii, Salvati, Thriplerex, CDMusic, psykomatic, & novaGnomes

Sprites: Heart Plus Up!, LordMetarex, Seasalt, toothbrush, IbanPlay, Cooper, & PokerFace
